Titanic

Titanic is an epic romance movie set against the maiden voyage of Titanic, the largest moving vessel ever built at that time. Titanic, dubbed as 'the ship of dreams' carried more than 1,500 people to their death on her first voyage in the North Atlantic Sea in 1912.

IT

As kids vanish throughout town, a group of outcasts must face their biggest fears as well as a murderous, terrifying and seemingly invincible clown. Watch all you want. Based on the acclaimed novel by Stephen King, this adaptation stars Bill Skarsgård as shape-shifting clown Pennywise.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Hustle

When a down-on-his-luck basketball scout finds a potential superstar in Spain, he sets out to prove they both have what it takes to make it in the NBA. Watch all you want. Adam Sandler teams up with Juancho Hernangómez in this rousing, feel-good film co-starring Queen Latifah and Robert Duvall.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Chickenhare

A brave young animal explorer teams up with two trusty friends to find a powerful artifact before his greedy uncle can get his paws on it. Watch all you want. This lighthearted comedy adventure with kid-friendly thrills is loosely based on the graphic novels by Chris Grine.

Speedkills

TIn the 1980s, a mobbed-up motorboat racer helps move millions of dollars' worth of drugs into Miami before drawing the ire of the DEA and the mafia. Watch all you want. John Travolta stars alongside Katheryn Winnick and Jennifer Esposito in this crime drama based on a true story.

The Amazing Spider-man

In this reboot of the hit superhero franchise, high schooler Peter Parker learns to wield his newfound powers while facing down arch-villain The Lizard. Watch all you want. Andrew Garfield stars in this web-slinging adventure alongside Emma Stone, Martin Sheen and Sally Field.
